WHAT IS CRYPTO ZHAO ?

Crypto Zhoa is a collection of unique NFT aspires to be a multi-chain collection, with each chain focusing on a different theme.

Crypto Zhao was created to recognize CZ (Changpeng Zhao) and his team's (Binance) efforts and contributions to the crypto industry and community. CZ is currently the role model of the crypto community, doing his best to defend cryptocurrency and improve its status and utilities.

"#bitcoin is not dead. We are still here."

-Changpeng Zhao

SUPERHERO EDITION | POLYGON

CRYPTO ZHAO SUPERHERO EDITION has a supply of 10,000 NFT.

Crypto Zhao's first collection is Superhero Edition. It is created to acknowledge that CZ is a hero to the crypto world. He is one of the saviors of this industry and a model figure, prioritizing honesty and transparency.

Aiming to become accessible to everyone, Crypto Zhao Superhero Edition is deployed at Polygon Network. With the thought of paying high gas fees removed, both new and experienced collectors can trade it with ease.
