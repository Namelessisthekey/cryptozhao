WHAT IS CRYPTO ZHAO ?

Crypto Zhao is a collection of unique NFTs created by White Fox Studio. It is a multi-chain collection with each chain focusing on a different Crypto Zhao theme.

Crypto Zhao currently has two PFP collections, which are deployed on the BNB Chain and Polygon Network. Each collections have different themed connected to the crypto world.

Our collection also resembles CZ (Changpeng Zhao), where our team gets inspired by his work focusing on saving and improving the Web3 and crypto world.

"#bitcoin is not dead. We are still here."

-Changpeng Zhao

CRYPTO ZHAO NFT EDITION

STEAMPUNK EDITION | BSC

Crypto Zhao - Steampunk Edition is a collection of 8,000 unique NFTs created by White Fox Studio. It symbolizes the continued growth of blockchain technologies through people's hard work. Steampunk Edition represents people's resilience to achieve technological advancement and their capacity to strive for positive outcomes despite challenging circumstances.

Steampunk Edition NFTs have nine parts with over a million possible unique combinations. It's deployed on the BNB Chain, where collectors can enjoy minting and trading with a minimal gas fee.

To improve Crypto Zhao's value, the minting cost will be doubled every time 1,000 NFT are minted. Good luck and have fun minting!
