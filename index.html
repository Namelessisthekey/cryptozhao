WHAT IS CRYPTO ZHAO ?

Crypto Zhoa is a collection of 10,000 unique NFTs dedicated to honor CZ (Changpeng Zhao). It was created to recognize CZ's efforts and contributions to the crypto industry and community.

He is currently the hero of the crypto community, who does his best to defend cryptocurrency and improve its status and utilities.

CRYPTO ZHAO NFTs

CRYPTO ZHAO has a limited supply of 10,000 NFT. You can use it as an avatar, or you can display it as a collection or as art.
